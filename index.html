about (in progress).

My goal with this project by the time of completion is to have a concept website to show how I would put together information for various sources such as lyric annotations from genius.com, sampling information from whosampled.com, and an analysis of the literary devices and rhyme scheme used in the songs by Kendrick Lamar over his past 3 albums.

Below are some wireframes I'm thinking for outlines for the section I'm currently thinking about, which is for analyzing samples. I'm thinking of 2 pie charts to show a breakdown of samples by decade and genre and then a list view to view the info of these samples.
